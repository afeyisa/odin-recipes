Chocolate

Description

Chocolate is a popular food product and ingredient that is made from cocoa beans. Cocoa beans are processed to extract cocoa solids and cocoa butter, which are the key components used to make chocolate. The process usually involves grinding the beans, adding sugar and often milk, and then conching, a process of heating and grinding, to achieve a smooth and consistent texture.

Ingredients

  • Cocoa beans
  • sugar
  • Coccoa butter
  • Milk solids
  • Emulsifiers
  • Flavoring

Steps

  1. Gather Ingredients
  2. Melt Chocolate
  3. Combine Wet and Dry Ingredients
  4. Mix Everything
  5. Preheat and cook
  6. Cool
  7. Serve or Store
